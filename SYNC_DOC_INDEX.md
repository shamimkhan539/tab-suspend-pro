## 💡 The Core Concept

### Simple Analogy:

Think of it like Gmail:

-   Gmail works on your home computer (shamim@gmail.com)
-   Gmail works on your work computer (same account)
-   Gmail works on your phone (same account)
-   All automatically synced

**Extension sync works the SAME way:**

-   Extension settings on home (shamim@gmail.com)
-   Extension settings on work (same account)
-   Extension settings on phone (same account)
-   All automatically synced

**Why?** Same Google account!
